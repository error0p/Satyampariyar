Mojo Pizza

An energetic, visually dominant campaign designed for Mojo Pizza’s signature "More Mojo" series. Highlighting fresh ingredients with dynamic typography and splashing visuals.

The Concept

High-energy typography meets splash-style food photography.

Consumer food campaigns need to trigger instant cravings. For Mojo Pizza, the challenge was to create a visual that breaks through busy social timelines. I paired bold, high-contrast, hand-carved styled typography ("MORE MOJO") with clean, high-speed action photography of pizza ingredients (green capsicums, sweet corn) floating around a hot, cheesy pizza slice.

The rich orange/red gradient background coordinates with the brand's identity while enhancing the warmth and flavor appeal of the food itself. A brush stroke label anchors the specific flavor variant: "Capsicum & Corn".
