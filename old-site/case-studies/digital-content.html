Campaign 01

5S: The Foundation of World-Class Manufacturing.

Lean manufacturing and workplace organization (5S) are crucial operational philosophies, but they are notoriously difficult to communicate dynamically on corporate feeds. The brief was to translate the technical Japanese principles (Sort, Set, Shine, Standardize, Sustain) into a compelling LinkedIn carousel.

The approach was to marry industrial aesthetics with strict design systems. Using high-contrast, black-and-white photography of real factory floors and workers, accented by a striking brand orange, each slide acts as a chapter in an engaging visual manual.

Campaign 02

Welcome Aboard: A Human-First Announcement.

Corporate welcoming posts are often cold, layout-heavy templates. To support Patil Group's rapid hiring cadence, I designed a reusable "Welcome Aboard" framework that puts the people first while preserving brand equity.

The layout uses warm cream backdrops and striking orange/red arched portrait frames. This provides an elegant editorial feel rather than a rigid corporate block, letting new employees across diverse divisions (Precast, Procurement, SCM, RMBD) feel celebrated in a premium unified visual system.

Welcome Aboard Campaign System

A repeatable, batch-friendly card grid built to scale. The design merges warm-toned editorial layouts with modern portrait geometry, setting an industrial standard for employee onboarding.
